# Business Impact

## Overview

The primary objective of the platform was to reduce manual effort associated with document review and claim validation while improving consistency, reliability, and business decision-making.

The solution was designed to address operational inefficiencies created by highly manual document-processing workflows involving claims, invoices, trade agreements, contracts, and supporting business documents.

Beyond document extraction, the platform helped accelerate validation workflows, reduce business risk, and establish a scalable foundation for future document intelligence initiatives.

---

# Business Problem Recap

Prior to implementation, business users spent significant time reviewing documents and gathering information required to determine whether claims were valid.

The process often involved:

* Reviewing supporting documents
* Searching across multiple systems
* Cross-referencing information
* Validating business rules
* Determining approval or rejection decisions

More than 250 business users were involved in these workflows.

Due to strict service-level agreements, documents could not always be fully validated before payment decisions were made, creating a risk of financial leakage.

# Quality Impact

## Improved Validation Consistency

Manual review outcomes can vary depending on reviewer experience and interpretation.

The platform introduced:

* Standardized extraction workflows
* Consistent validation rules
* Structured outputs
* Automated quality checks

This improved consistency across document-processing workflows.

---

## Reduced Hallucination Risk

Multiple controls were implemented to reduce unsupported extractions.

These included:

* Grounding on OCR content
* Structured outputs
* Validation layers
* Human review workflows
* Evidence-based extraction

This improved trustworthiness and reliability.

---

## Better Traceability

The platform retained:

* OCR outputs
* Validation outcomes
* Extraction results

This enabled teams to investigate issues and understand how outputs were generated.

---

## Improved Trust

Business users gained greater confidence in extraction results due to:

* Validation controls
* Transparency mechanisms
* Human review workflows
* Traceability

Trust was a critical factor in adoption.
